ABOUT TOURNAMENT

The Meghnad Saha Indoor Championship is the annual indoor gaming event of MSIT, Kolkata. This championship brings together the best talents to compete in four exciting indoor games – Chess, Badminton, Table Tennis, and Carrom. The tournament is designed to showcase skill, sportsmanship, and unity among students.

ABOUT MSIT

Meghnad Saha Institute of Technology (MSIT), located at Ruby, Kolkata – 700107, is one of the premier engineering institutions in West Bengal. The institute fosters academic excellence, innovation, and extracurricular activities, providing students with a holistic educational experience.

TOURNAMENT RULES

  • All participants must carry a valid college ID card.
  • Games will follow standard national rules for Chess, Badminton, Table Tennis, and Carrom.
  • Decisions of referees/umpires are final and binding.
  • Players must report to their matches on time, failing which walkover will be awarded.
  • Fair play and sportsmanship must be maintained at all times.
